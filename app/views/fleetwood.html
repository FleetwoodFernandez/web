Hunter Fleetwood

Hunter Fleetwood is a founding principal of Fleetwood / Fernandez, where he oversees the design direction and management of every project. His belief in the strength of ideas and a rigorous process guides a practice engaged in both construction and academia. With a background in fine arts, he spearheads the office's research efforts and public art projects.

Prior to founding Fleetwood / Fernandez, Hunter worked with Frederick Fisher and Partners for several years, where he completed award winning residential, commercial and museum projects. Hunter has lectured and served on juries at a number of educational institutions, and currently teaches undergraduate level studios and drawing at the University of Southern California School of architecture.

Hunter holds a B.A. in Architecture from Catholic University of America. He is a registered architect in California and New York. When not enthralled in architectural pursuits, he is most likely mountain biking, spoiling his wheaten terrier, drawing, or devouring whatever Paz is making that day.
