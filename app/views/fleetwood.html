Hunter Fleetwood

A founding principal of Fleetwood/ Fernandez, Hunter Fleetwood oversees the design direction and management of every project. His belief in the strength of ideas and a rigorous process guides a practice engaged in both construction and academia. With a background in fine arts, he spearheads the office’s research efforts and public art projects. Prior to founding Fleetwood/ Fernandez, Hunter worked with Frederick Fisher and Partners for several years, where he completed award winning residential, commercial and museum projects. Hunter has lectured and served on juries at a number of educational institutions, and currently teaches undergraduate level studios and drawing at the University of Southern California School of architecture. Hunter holds a B.A. in Architecture from Catholic University of America and he is a registered architect in California and New York.
